VIEWPORT
Question:
Shall I always use width=device-width?

Answer:
If your site is a fluid layout desktop design, use it is generally better when your site is viewed on mobile.
If your site is a fixed layout desktop design, it’s up to you, doesn’t matter.
If your site is a mobile specific design, just use it.



<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, target-densitydpi=160dpi, minimum-scale=1.0, maximum-scale=1.0, user-scalable=no">




MEDIA QUIERIES

@media only screen and (min-device-width : 320px) and (max-device-width : 569px) {...}





max-width and min-width are updated when you rotate the screen, your max-width in portrait mode become max-height in landscape mode
